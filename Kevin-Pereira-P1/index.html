Feijoada is Brazil's most loved dish

Feijoada (Brazilian Black Bean Stew) coonsits of stew of black beans with various types of port and beef. It is served with farofa, white rice, sauteed collard green and sliced oranges among other sides.

Price: $

Ingredients

  • 1 tablesoon olive oil
  • 1 and a half cups chopped onion, divided
  • 1 and a half cup green onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • 1 12 ounce package dry black beans, soaked overnight
  • 2 smoked ham hocks
  • 8 ounces diced ham
  • half pound thickly sliced bacon, diced
  • 2 bay leaves, crushed
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • half cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • one fourth chopped fresh parely
